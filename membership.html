Membership terms

The fine print.

A few things to know before you enroll. Questions? Email support@leapstrike.club.

  1. 01

    Billing & auto-renewal

    Dues are billed on the 1st of each month, and all memberships renew automatically.

  2. 02

    Payments

    All payments run through the CourtReserve app. Keep your payment details current there to avoid billing interruptions.

  3. 03

    Initiation fee

    The one-time initiation fee is non-refundable, including if you later cancel.

  4. 04

    Account requests

    Submit all account requests — changes, cancellations, holds — by email to support@leapstrike.club. Verbal requests can't be documented and won't be processed.

  5. 05

    Cancellation notice

    Cancellations require at least 30 days' written notice before your next billing date. Email support@leapstrike.club to cancel.

  6. 06

    Freeze or hold

    You may freeze or hold your membership for up to two months at no charge. Send freeze requests to support@leapstrike.club.

  7. 07

    Family & dependent members

    Spouses and children on a primary account must live at the same address as the primary member. We may refuse or revoke membership if valid ID confirming this isn't provided.

  8. 08

    Refunds

    No refund is issued on dues when a cancellation doesn't meet the 30-day notice requirement.

  9. 09

    No ID sharing

    Using another member's ID to enter the facility is fraud. It carries a $200 fine and may lead to permanent membership termination.

  10. 10

    Shuttles

    Bring your own shuttles. They're also available to buy at the club.

  11. 11

    Photo & video

    LeapStrike retains rights to all photos and video taken on the premises, including CCTV — used for security, live streaming and social media.

  12. 12

    Code of conduct

    Treat staff, coaches and fellow players with courtesy. Unsportsmanlike behavior isn't tolerated; we may warn, suspend or terminate membership without refund.

  13. 13

    Liability waiver

    Play carries an inherent risk of injury; you take part voluntarily and at your own risk. A separate waiver may be required at enrollment or check-in.
