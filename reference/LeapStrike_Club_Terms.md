# LeapStrike Club — Membership Terms, Court Rules & FAQ

---

## Membership Terms

1. **Billing:** Membership dues are charged on the 1st of each month. All memberships are enrolled in auto-renewal.
2. **Payments:** All membership payments are processed through the CourtReserve app. Members are responsible for keeping their payment information up to date in the app to avoid interruptions in billing.
3. **Initiation Fee:** The initiation fee is a one-time charge and is non-refundable, including upon account cancellation.
4. **Account Requests:** All account-related requests (changes, cancellations, holds, etc.) must be submitted by email to **support@leapstrike.club**. Verbal requests cannot be documented and will not be processed.
5. **Cancellation Notice:** Cancellations require at least 30 days' written notice before your next billing date. Email support@leapstrike.club to submit a cancellation request.
6. **Membership Freeze/Hold:** Members may freeze or place their membership on hold for up to two months at no charge. Freeze requests must be submitted by email to support@leapstrike.club.
7. **Reactivation Fees:**
   - Primary account: a $25 reactivation fee applies, after which the monthly plan resumes at $85/month.
   - Dependent account (spouse or child): a $15 reactivation fee applies.
8. **Family/Dependent Memberships:** Spouses and children added as secondary members on a primary account must reside at the same address as the primary member. The club reserves the right to refuse or revoke membership if valid ID confirming this is not provided.
9. **Refund:** No refunds will be issued on membership dues if a cancellation request does not meet the required 30-day notice period.


## Court Rules

1. **Check-In:** All players must check in at the front desk before play.
2. **Footwear:** Black-sole shoes and street shoes are not permitted in the gym. Non-marking indoor court shoes are required — badminton shoes are preferred. Entry will be denied for non-compliance, and session fees will not be refunded.
3. **Account Requests:** As above, all account-related requests must go through support@leapstrike.club; verbal requests will not be honored.
4. **ID Sharing:** Using another member's ID to enter the facility is considered fraud. This results in a $200 fine and may lead to permanent membership termination.
5. **Court Queuing:** During member hours, if demand exceeds available courts, players will be queued in the order they check in. (See Open Play Rules & Policy below for how queuing stands work during Open Play.)
6. **Session End:** Players must vacate the court when their open-play session ends, unless the next session has open slots.
7. **Singles Play:** Singles should be played on a singles court, or on a doubles court only if no other players are waiting for doubles.
8. **Shuttles:** Players are expected to bring their own shuttles. Shuttles are also available for purchase at the club.
9. **Cleanliness:** All participants must clean up their trash before leaving the facility.
10. **Photo/Video Rights:** LeapStrike retains full rights to all photos and video captured on the premises, including CCTV footage. This footage may be used for purposes including, but not limited to, security documentation, live streaming of matches, and social media content.
11. **Code of Conduct:** All members and guests are expected to treat staff, coaches, and fellow players with courtesy and respect at all times. Unsportsmanlike behavior — including verbal abuse, harassment, intimidation, intentional damage to equipment or facility property, or physical altercations — will not be tolerated. Disputes over calls or court use should be resolved calmly and, where needed, referred to front desk staff rather than settled between players. LeapStrike reserves the right to issue a warning, suspend, or permanently terminate the membership of anyone who violates this code of conduct, at the club's discretion and without refund.
12. **Liability Waiver:** Badminton and related physical activity carry an inherent risk of injury. By entering the facility and participating in play, members and guests acknowledge this risk and agree that they do so voluntarily and at their own risk. LeapStrike, its owners, staff, and affiliates are not liable for any injury, loss, or damage to person or property arising from participation in club activities, except where caused by the club's gross negligence. All members and guests may be required to sign a separate liability waiver upon enrollment or check-in.

## Open Play Rules & Policy

1. **Queuing Stands:** The club uses a system of stands to manage court queuing. Each stand holds a maximum of 4 rackets and represents one queue. If a stand already has 4 rackets, place your racket in the next available stand.
2. **Next-Up Marker:** A marker indicates which stand is "next available" — the group in that stand plays as soon a court opens up.
3. **Waiting for a Game to End:** Players in queue must wait for the current game to finish. Once a game ends, players in queue may ask the players on court to vacate.
4. **One Racket Per Player:** To join a queue, place your racket in one of the stands — one racket per player, maximum. Players may not reserve a spot for another player who is not present.
5. **No Double-Queuing:** A player currently on a court may not also have a racket in a stand. You cannot be playing and waiting in queue at the same time.
6. **Choosing a Stand:** Players may choose which stand to place their racket in, up to the 4-racket limit per stand. Players who want to play together may place their rackets in the same stand.
7. **Don't Move Others' Rackets:** Never move another player's racket from one stand to another without that player's consent.
8. **Groups of Four:** Only complete groups of 4 players may take an available court. It's the group's responsibility to have all 4 players assembled by the time their turn comes up.
9. **Incomplete Groups:** A group that isn't complete when its turn arrives forfeits that turn until it has assembled 4 players. An incomplete group may invite players coming off the court (the court the group is about to take) to join and complete the group.
10. **Withdrawing a Racket:** Players may withdraw their racket from a stand at any time. If withdrawing from a stand that had 4 rackets, the remaining players should find a replacement to keep the group complete. A player may not withdraw their racket once the group is in the process of moving onto a vacant court, as this would leave the group incomplete and forfeit its turn.
11. **Joining a Partial Stand:** Any player may join a stand that currently has 3 or fewer rackets.
12. **Cancellations & Refunds:** Players who need to cancel their Open Play registration may withdraw at least 4 hours in advance to receive a refund.
13. **Advancing the Marker:** When a group moves from its stand onto an available court, the "next available" marker moves to the next higher-numbered stand (or back to stand 1 if the highest-numbered stand was just used).

## FAQ

**Do I have to be a member to play?**
No. Drop-in guests are welcome and can purchase a day pass at the front desk for $15.

**I have a membership — do I need to register for Open Play?**
Yes. Both members and non-members must register in advance for Open Play and check in at the front desk to participate.

**Do I need to bring my own racket and shuttlecocks? What else should I bring?**
You're welcome to bring your own equipment. If you don't have a racket, rentals are available starting at $2, and shuttlecocks can be purchased on-site. A Pro Shop is also available if you'd like to buy your own gear.

**What shoes are allowed?**
Only non-marking shoes are permitted; black-sole shoes are not allowed. Players wearing shoes that leave marks on the court will be fined $100.

**How do I change my membership or training program?**
Membership changes must be requested by email to support@leapstrike.club. Please allow 1–4 business days for the change to be processed.

**How do I cancel my membership or training program?**
Cancellations require 30 days' advance notice. Email support@leapstrike.club to submit your cancellation.

**What if I need to reach the club for something not covered here?**
Please email support@leapstrike.club for ant inquiries
