Court rules

Play fair, play safe.

These rules keep courts fast, fair and in great condition for everyone.

  1. 01

    Check in first

    Check in at the front desk before you play.

  2. 02

    Footwear

    Non-marking indoor court shoes are required — badminton shoes preferred. Black-sole and street shoes aren't allowed; entry is denied for non-compliance and session fees aren't refunded.

  3. 03

    Account requests

    All account requests go through support@leapstrike.club; verbal requests won't be honored.

  4. 04

    No ID sharing

    Using another member's ID to enter is fraud. It carries a $200 fine and may lead to permanent termination.

  5. 05

    Court queuing

    During member hours, if demand exceeds courts, players are queued in check-in order. See the Open Play policy for how queuing stands work.

  6. 06

    Session end

    Vacate the court when your open-play session ends, unless the next session still has open slots.

  7. 07

    Singles play

    Play singles on a singles court — or on a doubles court only if no one is waiting for doubles.

  8. 08

    Shuttles

    Bring your own shuttles. They're also available to buy at the club.

  9. 09

    Cleanliness

    Clean up your trash before you leave the facility.

  10. 10

    Photo & video

    LeapStrike retains rights to all photos and video taken on the premises, including CCTV — used for security, live streaming and social media.

  11. 11

    Code of conduct

    Treat staff, coaches and fellow players with courtesy. Unsportsmanlike behavior isn't tolerated; we may warn, suspend or terminate membership without refund.

  12. 12

    Liability waiver

    Play carries an inherent risk of injury; you take part voluntarily and at your own risk. A separate waiver may be required at enrollment or check-in.
