Training programs

Get better, faster.

Coaching for every level, from your first grip to tournament match play. Group clinics, an age-banded junior pathway, competitive squads, and one-on-one sessions.

01

Foundations · Beginner

Grips, footwork and rally basics in a friendly small-group setting. Eight-week block, no experience needed.

02

Junior Academy · Ages 7–16

An age-banded pathway building technique, fitness and match play. Runs after school and on weekends.

03

Competitive Squad

High-intensity drills, tactics and sparring for tournament players. Places are coach-selected.

04

Private 1-on-1

Personalised sessions with a certified coach, with video analysis available. Book by the hour.
